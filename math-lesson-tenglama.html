Equations ⚖️

Atamalar → Formulalar → 5 ta yechilgan misol. Har bir qadamni tushunib o'rganing.

1-bosqich

Atamalar 📖

Equation / Tenglama
Tenglik belgisi bo'lgan ifoda
Ikkita ifodani tenglik belgisi (=) orqali bog'laydigan yozuv. Masalan: \(2x + 3 = 7\)
Variable / O'zgaruvchi
Noma'lum son
Qiymatini topishimiz kerak bo'lgan harf. Odatda \(x, y, z\) harflari ishlatiladi.
Coefficient / Koeffitsient
O'zgaruvchi oldidagi son
\(5x\) da koeffitsient — \(5\). \(-3y\) da koeffitsient — \(-3\).
Constant / Doimiy son
O'zgarmaydigan son
\(2x + 7 = 15\) da \(7\) va \(15\) — doimiy sonlar.
Root / Ildiz (yechim)
Tenglamaning javob (yechimi)
Tenglama to'g'ri bo'lishi uchun \(x\) ning qiymati. \(x = 2\) — bu tenglama ildizi.
Linear equation
Chiziqli tenglama (1-daraja)
O'zgaruvchi birinchi darajada bo'lgan tenglama: \(ax + b = c\)
Quadratic equation
Kvadrat tenglama (2-daraja)
O'zgaruvchi ikkinchi darajada bo'lgan tenglama: \(ax^2 + bx + c = 0\)
Discriminant / Diskriminant
Tenglamaning yechim soni aniqlovchisi
\(D = b^2 - 4ac\). \(D > 0\): 2 ildiz, \(D = 0\): 1 ildiz, \(D < 0\): ildiz yo'q.
Transpose / Ko'chirish
Tenglik belgisidan o'tkazish
Sonni bir tomondan ikkinchisiga o'tkazganda ishorasi o'zgaradi: \(+\) → \(-\), \(\times\) → \(\div\)
Absolute value / Modul
Soning musbat qiymati
\(|x|\) — har doim musbat: \(|-5| = 5\), \(|3| = 3\), \(|0| = 0\)
System of equations
Tenglamalar sistemasi
Bir vaqtda hal qilinadigan bir nechta tenglama. Masalan: \(\begin{cases} x + y = 5 \\ x - y = 1 \end{cases}\)
Solution set / Yechimlar to'plami
Barcha to'g'ri javoblar
Tenglamani qoniqtiradigan barcha \(x\) qiymatlari to'plami.

2-bosqich

Formulalar 📐

Chiziqli tenglama — Linear Equation
\[ ax + b = c \implies x = \frac{c - b}{a} \]
a — koeffitsient (0 bo'lmasligi kerak)  |  b — doimiy son  |  c — o'ng tomon  |  x — noma'lum
Kvadrat tenglama — Quadratic Equation
\[ ax^2 + bx + c = 0 \]
\[ D = b^2 - 4ac \]
\[ x_{1,2} = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{D}}{2a} \]
D > 0 → 2 ta haqiqiy ildiz  |  D = 0 → 1 ta ildiz  |  D < 0 → haqiqiy ildiz yo'q
Viyeta teoremasi — Vieta's Formulas
\[ x_1 + x_2 = -\frac{b}{a} \qquad x_1 \cdot x_2 = \frac{c}{a} \]
Ildizlarning yig'indisi va ko'paytmasi orqali tenglamani tezroq yechish mumkin.
Modul tenglamasi — Absolute Value Equation
\[ |x| = a \implies x = a \;\; \text{yoki} \;\; x = -a \;\; (a \geq 0) \]
\[ |f(x)| = g(x) \implies f(x) = g(x) \;\; \text{yoki} \;\; f(x) = -g(x) \]
|x| = a da a < 0 bo'lsa yechim yo'q.
Tenglamalar sistemasi — System (elimination usuli)
\[ \begin{cases} a_1x + b_1y = c_1 \\ a_2x + b_2y = c_2 \end{cases} \]
Qo'shish usuli: Bitta o'zgaruvchini yo'q qilish uchun tenglamalarni qo'shing yoki ayiring.
Almashtirish usuli: Bitta tenglamadan \(x\) ni topib, ikkinchisiga qo'ying.

3-bosqich

Yechilgan misollar 🧮

Har bir misol ustiga bosing — bosqichma-bosqich yechimni ko'ring.

1
\(3x + 7 = 22\) tenglamasini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Doimiy sonni o'ng tomonga o'tkazing
\(3x = 22 - 7\)
\(+7\) ni chap tomondan o'ng tomonga o'tkazganda \(-7\) ga aylanadi.
2
O'ng tomondagi hisoblash
\(3x = 15\)
\(22 - 7 = 15\)
3
Koeffitsientga bo'ling
\(x = \dfrac{15}{3}\)
\(x\) ni topish uchun ikkala tomonni \(3\) ga bo'lamiz.
4
Tekshirish
\(3 \cdot 5 + 7 = 15 + 7 = 22 \checkmark\)
Javobni asl tenglamaga qo'yib tekshiramiz — to'g'ri!
JAVOB: \(x = 5\)
2
\(x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0\) tenglamasini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Koeffitsientlarni aniqlaymiz
\(a = 1, \quad b = -5, \quad c = 6\)
Standart shakl: \(ax^2 + bx + c = 0\)
2
Diskriminant hisoblaymiz
\(D = b^2 - 4ac = (-5)^2 - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot 6\)
\(D = 25 - 24 = 1\)
\(D > 0\) bo'lgani uchun 2 ta haqiqiy ildiz mavjud.
3
Ildizlarni hisoblaymiz
\(x_{1,2} = \dfrac{-b \pm \sqrt{D}}{2a} = \dfrac{5 \pm \sqrt{1}}{2}\)
\(x_1 = \dfrac{5 + 1}{2} = 3 \qquad x_2 = \dfrac{5 - 1}{2} = 2\)
4
Viyeta bilan tekshirish
\(x_1 + x_2 = 3 + 2 = 5 = -\dfrac{b}{a} = -\dfrac{-5}{1} \checkmark\)
\(x_1 \cdot x_2 = 3 \cdot 2 = 6 = \dfrac{c}{a} = \dfrac{6}{1} \checkmark\)
JAVOB: \(x_1 = 3, \quad x_2 = 2\)
💡 Tezroq usul: \(x^2 - 5x + 6 = (x-2)(x-3) = 0\) deb ham yechish mumkin. Ko'paytuvchilardan biri \(0\) bo'lishi kerak.
3
\(4x^2 - 12x + 9 = 0\) tenglamasini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Koeffitsientlar
\(a = 4, \quad b = -12, \quad c = 9\)
2
Diskriminant
\(D = (-12)^2 - 4 \cdot 4 \cdot 9 = 144 - 144 = 0\)
\(D = 0\) bo'lgani uchun faqat 1 ta ildiz bor (qo'sh ildiz).
3
Ildizni hisoblaymiz
\(x = \dfrac{-b}{2a} = \dfrac{12}{8} = \dfrac{3}{2}\)
4
To'liq kvadrat ko'rinishi
\(4x^2 - 12x + 9 = (2x - 3)^2 = 0\)
Bu tenglama to'liq kvadrat — shu sababli faqat bitta ildiz.
JAVOB: \(x = \dfrac{3}{2} = 1.5\)
4
\(|2x - 3| = 7\) tenglamasini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Modul tenglamasini ochish
\(|2x - 3| = 7\)
Modul ichidagi ifoda \(+7\) yoki \(-7\) bo'lishi mumkin.
2
1-holat: ichki ifoda musbat
\(2x - 3 = 7\)
\(2x = 10 \implies x_1 = 5\)
3
2-holat: ichki ifoda manfiy
\(2x - 3 = -7\)
\(2x = -4 \implies x_2 = -2\)
4
Tekshirish
\(|2(5) - 3| = |7| = 7 \checkmark\)
\(|2(-2) - 3| = |-7| = 7 \checkmark\)
JAVOB: \(x_1 = 5, \quad x_2 = -2\)
5
Sistemani yeching: \(\begin{cases} 2x + 3y = 12 \\ x - y = 1 \end{cases}\)
👁 Ko'rish
1
Almashtirish usuli — 2-tenglamadan x ni topamiz
\(x - y = 1 \implies x = y + 1\)
2-tenglamani \(x\) ga nisbatan yozamiz.
2
1-tenglamaga x ni qo'yish
\(2(y + 1) + 3y = 12\)
\(2y + 2 + 3y = 12\)
\(5y = 10 \implies y = 2\)
3
x ni topamiz
\(x = y + 1 = 2 + 1 = 3\)
4
Tekshirish — ikkala tenglamaga qo'yamiz
\(2(3) + 3(2) = 6 + 6 = 12 \checkmark\)
\(3 - 2 = 1 \checkmark\)
JAVOB: \(x = 3, \quad y = 2\)
💡 Qo'shish usuli bilan ham yechib ko'ring: 2-tenglamani 3 ga ko'paytirib, 1-tenglamadan ayiring. Natija bir xil bo'ladi!
6
\(\sqrt{2x + 3} = x - 1\) tenglamasini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Mavjudlik sharti
\(2x + 3 \geq 0 \Rightarrow x \geq -1.5\) va \(x - 1 \geq 0 \Rightarrow x \geq 1\)
Ildizdan o'ng tomon ham manfiy bo'lmasligi kerak.
2
Ikkala tomonni kvadratga oshiramiz
\(2x + 3 = (x-1)^2 = x^2 - 2x + 1\)
3
Tenglamani tartiblang
\(x^2 - 4x - 2 = 0\)
\(D = 16 + 8 = 24,\quad x = \frac{4 \pm \sqrt{24}}{2} = 2 \pm \sqrt{6}\)
4
Shart tekshirish: \(x \geq 1\)
\(x_1 = 2 + \sqrt{6} \approx 4.45 \geq 1 \checkmark\)
\(x_2 = 2 - \sqrt{6} \approx -0.45 < 1 \times\)
x₂ shartni qoniqtirmaydi — chiqarib tashlaymiz.
JAVOB: \(x = 2 + \sqrt{6}\)
💡 Diqqat: Ildizli tenglamalarda ALBATTA tekshirish kerak — kvadratga oshirganda yolg'on ildizlar paydo bo'lishi mumkin!
7
\(\dfrac{x}{x-2} + \dfrac{3}{x+2} = \dfrac{x^2+4}{x^2-4}\) tenglamasini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Mahraj shartlari: \(x \neq 2\) va \(x \neq -2\)
\(x^2 - 4 = (x-2)(x+2)\)
2
Umumiy mahrajga keltiramiz: \((x-2)(x+2)\)
\(x(x+2) + 3(x-2) = x^2 + 4\)
3
Ochib yozamiz
\(x^2 + 2x + 3x - 6 = x^2 + 4\)
\(5x - 6 = 4 \Rightarrow 5x = 10 \Rightarrow x = 2\)
4
Tekshirish: \(x = 2\) mahraj shartini buzadi!
\(x = 2 \Rightarrow x - 2 = 0 \Rightarrow \text{Mahraj nolga teng!}\)
Shuning uchun yechim yo'q.
JAVOB: Yechim yo'q (∅)
💡 Kasrli tenglamada: Mahraj nolga teng bo'lgan qiymatlar HECH QACHON yechim bo'la olmaydi!
8
\(x^2 - 6x + (k+2) = 0\) tenglamasi faqat bitta ildizga ega bo'lishi uchun \(k\) ni toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Bitta ildiz → D = 0
\(D = b^2 - 4ac = 0\)
2
Koeffitsientlar: \(a=1, b=-6, c=k+2\)
\((-6)^2 - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot (k+2) = 0\)
\(36 - 4k - 8 = 0\)
3
k ni topamiz
\(28 = 4k \Rightarrow k = 7\)
4
Tekshirish: \(k=7\) da ildiz
\(x = \frac{-b}{2a} = \frac{6}{2} = 3\)
\(9 - 18 + 9 = 0 \checkmark\)
JAVOB: \(k = 7\)
9
\(2^{x+1} - 2^x = 16\) tenglamasini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
\(2^{x+1}\) ni ajratamiz
\(2^{x+1} = 2^x \cdot 2^1 = 2 \cdot 2^x\)
2
Umumiy ko'paytuvchiga chiqaramiz
\(2 \cdot 2^x - 2^x = 16\)
\(2^x(2 - 1) = 16\)
\(2^x = 16\)
3
\(16 = 2^4\)
\(2^x = 2^4 \Rightarrow x = 4\)
JAVOB: \(x = 4\)
💡 Usul: Ko'rsatkichli tenglamalarda asoslarni tenglashtirib, keyin ko'rsatkichlarni tenglashtiring.
10
Sistemani yeching: \(\begin{cases} x + y + z = 6 \\ x - y + z = 2 \\ 2x + y - z = 1 \end{cases}\)
👁 Ko'rish
1
1 va 2 tenglamani qo'shish (y yo'qoladi)
\((x+y+z) + (x-y+z) = 6 + 2\)
\(2x + 2z = 8 \Rightarrow x + z = 4 \quad \cdots (I)\)
2
2 va 3 tenglamani qo'shish
\((x-y+z) + (2x+y-z) = 2 + 1\)
\(3x = 3 \Rightarrow x = 1\)
3
(I) dan z ni topamiz
\(1 + z = 4 \Rightarrow z = 3\)
4
1-tenglamadan y ni topamiz
\(1 + y + 3 = 6 \Rightarrow y = 2\)
5
Tekshirish — 3-tenglamaga qo'yamiz
\(2(1) + 2 - 3 = 2 + 2 - 3 = 1 \checkmark\)
JAVOB: \(x = 1,\quad y = 2,\quad z = 3\)
