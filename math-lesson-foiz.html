Foiz va Nisbat
Percentage & Ratio 💯

Atamalar → Formulalar → 10 ta yechilgan misol. Har bir qadamni tushunib o'rganing.

1-bosqich

Atamalar 📖

Percentage / Foiz
Yuzdan bir qism
\(1\% = \frac{1}{100} = 0.01\). Masalan: \(25\% = 0.25\)
Base / Asos
Foiz hisoblanadigan son
\(30\%\) of \(200\) da asos = \(200\)
Rate / Stavka
Foiz miqdori
\(30\%\) da stavka = \(30\)
Percentage of a number
Sonning foizi
\(p\%\) of \(N = \frac{p \cdot N}{100}\)
Percentage increase
Foiz o'sish
\(\frac{\text{yangi} - \text{eski}}{\text{eski}} \times 100\%\)
Percentage decrease
Foiz kamayish
\(\frac{\text{eski} - \text{yangi}}{\text{eski}} \times 100\%\)
Ratio / Nisbat
Ikki sonning munosabati
\(a:b = \frac{a}{b}\). Masalan: \(3:4\)
Proportion / Proporsiya
Ikki nisbat tengligI
\(\frac{a}{b} = \frac{c}{d} \Rightarrow ad = bc\)
Simple interest
Oddiy foiz
\(I = P \cdot r \cdot t\)
Compound interest
Murakkab foiz
\(A = P(1 + r)^t\)
Discount / Chegirma
Narxdan kamaytirish
Asl narx × (1 - discount rate)
Markup / Ustama
Narxga qo'shish
Tannarx × (1 + markup rate)

2-bosqich

Formulalar 📐

Foiz hisoblash
\[P = \frac{\text{qism}}{\text{butun}} \times 100\%\]
\[\text{qism} = \frac{P \times \text{butun}}{100}\]
\[\text{butun} = \frac{\text{qism} \times 100}{P}\]
Uchta formuladan qaysi ikkisi berilgan bo'lsa, uchinchisini topasiz.
Foiz o'zgarish
\[\text{o'sish \%} = \frac{\text{yangi} - \text{eski}}{\text{eski}} \times 100\%\]
\[\text{yangi qiymat} = \text{eski} \times \left(1 + \frac{P}{100}\right)\]
Nisbat va proporsiya
\[a : b = c : d \Rightarrow \frac{a}{b} = \frac{c}{d} \Rightarrow ad = bc\]
\[\text{Nisbatda bo'lish: } a : b \Rightarrow \text{birinchi qism} = \frac{a}{a+b} \times S\]
Oddiy va Murakkab foiz
\[\text{Oddiy: } A = P(1 + rt)\]
\[\text{Murakkab: } A = P\left(1 + \frac{r}{n}\right)^{nt}\]
P — boshlang'ich summa  |  r — yillik foiz stavkasi  |  t — yil soni  |  n — yilda hisoblash soni

3-bosqich

Yechilgan misollar 🧮

Har bir misol ustiga bosing — bosqichma-bosqich yechimni ko'ring.

1
\(250\) sonining \(40\%\) ini toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Formuladan:
\(\frac{40 \times 250}{100}\)
2
Hisob
\(= \frac{10000}{100} = 100\)
JAVOB:\(100\)
2
Qaysi sonning \(30\%\) i — \(75\)?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Butun = qism × 100 / P
\(\text{butun} = \frac{75 \times 100}{30}\)
2
Hisob
\(= \frac{7500}{30} = 250\)
JAVOB:\(250\)
3
Narx \(1200\) so'mdan \(1500\) so'mga o'sdi. Necha foizga o'sdi?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Foiz o'sish formulasi
\(\frac{1500 - 1200}{1200} \times 100\%\)
2
Hisob
\(= \frac{300}{1200} \times 100\% = 25\%\)
JAVOB:\(25\%\)
4
Telefon narxi \(20\%\) chegirmadan keyin \(960\) so'm bo'ldi. Asl narxi nima edi?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Chegirmadan keyin narx = asl × 0.80
\(\text{asl} \times 0.80 = 960\)
2
Yechamiz
\(\text{asl} = \frac{960}{0.80} = 1200 \text{ so'm}\)
JAVOB:\(1200 \text{ so'm}\)
5
\(a : b = 3 : 5\) va \(a + b = 48\). \(a\) va \(b\) ni toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
a ning ulushi:
\(a = \frac{3}{3+5} \times 48 = \frac{3}{8} \times 48 = 18\)
2
b ni topamiz:
\(b = 48 - 18 = 30\)
3
Tekshirish:
\(18 : 30 = 3 : 5 \checkmark\)
JAVOB:\(a = 18,\; b = 30\)
6
\(\frac{x}{4} = \frac{6}{8}\) proporsiyasidan \(x\) ni toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Kresт ko'paytirish (cross multiply):
\(8x = 4 \times 6 = 24\)
2
Yechamiz:
\(x = \frac{24}{8} = 3\)
JAVOB:\(x = 3\)
7
Bankka \(P = 10000\) so'm qo'yildi. Yillik \(r = 15\%\), \(t = 3\) yil. Oddiy foiz bilan qancha bo'ladi?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Formuladan:
\(A = P(1 + rt) = 10000(1 + 0.15 \times 3)\)
2
Hisob:
\(A = 10000(1 + 0.45) = 10000 \times 1.45\)
3
Natija:
\(A = 14500 \text{ so'm}\)
JAVOB:\(14500 \text{ so'm}\)
8
Murakkab foiz: \(P = 5000\), \(r = 10\%\), \(t = 2\) yil. Oxirgi summa?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Formuladan:
\(A = 5000(1 + 0.10)^2\)
2
Hisob:
\(A = 5000 \times 1.1^2 = 5000 \times 1.21\)
3
Natija:
\(A = 6050 \text{ so'm}\)
Oddiy foizdan (6000 so'm) ko'proq!
JAVOB:\(6050 \text{ so'm}\)
9
Bir mahsulot tannarxi \(800\) so'm, ustama \(25\%\). Sotish narxi?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Formuladan:
\(\text{narx} = 800 \times (1 + 0.25)\)
2
Hisob:
\(= 800 \times 1.25 = 1000 \text{ so'm}\)
JAVOB:\(1000 \text{ so'm}\)
10
Sinfdagi 25 o'quvchining 60% qizlar. Qancha o'g'il bola bor?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Qizlar soni:
\(0.60 \times 25 = 15 \text{ qiz}\)
2
O'g'il bolalar:
\(25 - 15 = 10\)
3
Tekshirish:
\(\frac{10}{25} \times 100\% = 40\%\checkmark\)
JAVOB:\(10 \text{ o'g'il bola}\)
