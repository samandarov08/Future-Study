Geometry 📐

Atamalar → Formulalar → 10 ta yechilgan misol. Har bir qadamni tushunib o'rganing.

1-bosqich

Atamalar 📖

Triangle / Uchburchak
3 ta tomon va 3 ta burchak
Burchaklar yig'indisi \(180°\).
Right triangle
To'g'ri burchakli uchburchak
Bir burchagi \(90°\). Gipotenuza — eng uzun tomon.
Pythagorean theorem
Pifagor teoremasi
\(a^2 + b^2 = c^2\) — to'g'ri burchakli uchburchakda.
Area / Yuza
Shaklning o'lchanadigan ichi
Kvadrat birliklarda o'lchanadi: cm², m²
Perimeter / Perimetr
Shaklning tashqi chegarasi uzunligi
Barcha tomonlar yig'indisi.
Circle / Aylana
Bir nuqtadan teng masofadagi nuqtalar to'plami
\(r\) — radius, \(d = 2r\) — diametr
Circumference
Aylana uzunligi
\(C = 2\pi r = \pi d\)
Sector / Sektor
Aylana bo'lagi
Pizza dilimi kabi: \(A = \frac{\theta}{360°} \pi r^2\)
Parallelogram
Qarama-qarshi tomonlari parallel to'rtburchak
\(A = b \times h\)
Trapezoid / Trapetsiya
Bir jufti parallel tomonli to'rtburchak
\(A = \frac{(a+b)}{2} \times h\)
Similar triangles
O'xshash uchburchaklar
Burchaklari teng, tomonlari proporsional.
Coordinate geometry
Koordinata geometriyasi
Shakllarni koordinata sistemasida o'rganish.

2-bosqich

Formulalar 📐

Uchburchak
\[S = \frac{1}{2} \times a \times h_a\]
\[S = \frac{1}{2}ab\sin C\]
\[S = \sqrt{p(p-a)(p-b)(p-c)}, \quad p = \frac{a+b+c}{2} \text{ (Geron)}\]
\[a^2 = b^2 + c^2 - 2bc\cos A \quad \text{(Kosinuslar teoremasi)}\]
\[\frac{a}{\sin A} = \frac{b}{\sin B} = \frac{c}{\sin C} \quad \text{(Sinuslar teoremasi)}\]
To'rtburchaklar
\[\text{Kvadrat: } S = a^2, \; P = 4a\]
\[\text{To'g'ri burchakli: } S = ab, \; P = 2(a+b)\]
\[\text{Parallelogramm: } S = ah\]
\[\text{Trapetsiya: } S = \frac{(a+b)h}{2}\]
\[\text{Romb: } S = \frac{d_1 \cdot d_2}{2}\]
Aylana
\[C = 2\pi r, \quad S_{\text{disk}} = \pi r^2\]
\[S_{\text{sektor}} = \frac{\alpha}{360°} \pi r^2 = \frac{1}{2}r^2\alpha_{\text{rad}}\]
\[l_{\text{yoy}} = \frac{\alpha}{360°} \cdot 2\pi r\]
Koordinata geometriyasi
\[d = \sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2 + (y_2-y_1)^2} \quad \text{(masofa)}\]
\[M = \left(\frac{x_1+x_2}{2}, \frac{y_1+y_2}{2}\right) \quad \text{(o'rta nuqta)}\]

3-bosqich

Yechilgan misollar 🧮

Har bir misol ustiga bosing — bosqichma-bosqich yechimni ko'ring.

1
To'g'ri burchakli uchburchak: katetlar \(a=3\), \(b=4\). Gipotenuza \(c\)?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Pifagor teoremasi:
\(c^2 = a^2 + b^2 = 9 + 16 = 25\)
2
\(c = \sqrt{25} = 5\)
JAVOB:\(c = 5\)
2
Tomonlari \(5, 12, 13\) bo'lgan uchburchak to'g'ri burchaklimi?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Pifagor: eng katta tomon — 13
\(5^2 + 12^2 = 25 + 144 = 169 = 13^2 \checkmark\)
2
Xulosa:
\(\text{Ha, to'g'ri burchakli uchburchak.}\)
JAVOB:Ha, to'g'ri burchakli
3
Geron formulasi: \(a=5, b=6, c=7\). Yuza?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Yarim perimetr:
\(p = \frac{5+6+7}{2} = 9\)
2
Formuladan:
\(S = \sqrt{9(9-5)(9-6)(9-7)} = \sqrt{9 \cdot 4 \cdot 3 \cdot 2}\)
3
Hisob:
\(S = \sqrt{216} = 6\sqrt{6} \approx 14.7\)
JAVOB:\(6\sqrt{6} \approx 14.7\)
4
Radius \(r = 7\) sm bo'lgan aylananing uzunligi va yuzini toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Uzunlik:
\(C = 2\pi r = 2\pi \times 7 = 14\pi \approx 43.98 \text{ sm}\)
2
Yuza:
\(S = \pi r^2 = 49\pi \approx 153.94 \text{ sm}^2\)
JAVOB:\(C = 14\pi,\quad S = 49\pi\)
5
Trapetsiya: parallel tomonlar \(a=8\), \(b=12\), balandlik \(h=5\). Yuza?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Formuladan:
\(S = \frac{(a+b)h}{2} = \frac{(8+12) \times 5}{2}\)
2
Hisob:
\(S = \frac{20 \times 5}{2} = 50\)
JAVOB:\(S = 50\)
6
Parallelogrammning asosi \(b=10\), balandligi \(h=6\). Rombning diagonallari \(d_1=8, d_2=10\). Qaysi biri katta?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Parallelogramm:
\(S = 10 \times 6 = 60\)
2
Romb:
\(S = \frac{8 \times 10}{2} = 40\)
3
Taqqoslash:
\(60 > 40\)
Parallelogramm katta.
JAVOB:Parallelogramm yuzasi katta: \(60 > 40\)
7
\(A(1,2)\) va \(B(5,5)\) nuqtalar orasidagi masofa?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Formuladan:
\(d = \sqrt{(5-1)^2 + (5-2)^2}\)
2
Hisob:
\(d = \sqrt{16 + 9} = \sqrt{25} = 5\)
JAVOB:\(d = 5\)
8
\(A(2,4)\) va \(B(8,12)\) ning o'rta nuqtasini toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Formuladan:
\(M = \left(\frac{2+8}{2}, \frac{4+12}{2}\right)\)
2
Hisob:
\(M = (5, 8)\)
JAVOB:\(M = (5,\ 8)\)
9
Sektor burchagi \(90°\), radius \(r=6\). Sektor yuzi?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Formuladan:
\(S = \frac{90}{360} \times \pi \times 6^2\)
2
Hisob:
\(S = \frac{1}{4} \times 36\pi = 9\pi \approx 28.27\)
JAVOB:\(S = 9\pi\)
10
Kosinuslar teoremasi: \(b=5, c=7, A=60°\). \(a\) ni toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Formuladan:
\(a^2 = b^2 + c^2 - 2bc\cos A\)
2
\(a^2 = 25 + 49 - 2(5)(7)\cos 60°\)
3
\(\cos 60° = 0.5\):
\(a^2 = 74 - 70(0.5) = 74 - 35 = 39\)
4
Natija:
\(a = \sqrt{39} \approx 6.24\)
JAVOB:\(a = \sqrt{39} \approx 6.24\)
