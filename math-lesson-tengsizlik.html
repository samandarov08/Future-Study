Tengsizlik
Inequalities ⚖️

Atamalar → Formulalar → 10 ta yechilgan misol. Har bir qadamni tushunib o'rganing.

1-bosqich

Atamalar 📖

Inequality / Tengsizlik
Tenglik bo'lmagan ifoda
Ikki ifodani >, <, ≥, ≤ belgisi bilan bog'laydigan yozuv. Masalan: \(2x + 1 > 5\)
Greater than / Katta
\(a > b\)
a b dan katta. Masalan: \(7 > 3\)
Less than / Kichik
\(a < b\)
a b dan kichik. Masalan: \(2 < 9\)
Greater or equal / Katta yoki teng
\(a \geq b\)
a b dan katta yoki unga teng.
Less or equal / Kichik yoki teng
\(a \leq b\)
a b dan kichik yoki unga teng.
Number line / Son o'qi
Javobni ko'rsatish usuli
Tengsizlik yechimi son o'qida interval sifatida ko'rsatiladi.
Interval / Interval
Yechimlar to'plami
\((a,b)\) — ochiq, \([a,b]\) — yopiq interval. \((2, +\infty)\) — 2 dan katta sonlar.
Flip the sign / Ishora o'zgarishi
Manfiy songa ko'paytirganda
Manfiy songa ko'paytirish yoki bo'lganda \(>\) ↔ \(<\) almashadi!
Compound inequality
Ikki tengsizlik birlashgan
\(a < x < b\) — x a dan katta va b dan kichik.
Quadratic inequality
Kvadrat tengsizlik
\(ax^2 + bx + c > 0\) yoki \(< 0\) ko'rinishidagi tengsizlik.
Rational inequality
Kasrli tengsizlik
\(\frac{f(x)}{g(x)} > 0\) ko'rinishidagi tengsizlik.
Absolute inequality
Modul tengsizligi
\(|x| < a \Rightarrow -a < x < a\)

2-bosqich

Formulalar 📐

Asosiy qoidalar
\[a > b \Rightarrow a + c > b + c\]
\[a > b,\; c > 0 \Rightarrow ac > bc\]
\[a > b,\; c < 0 \Rightarrow ac < bc \;\text{(ishora o'zgaradi!)}\]
Muhim: Manfiy songa ko'paytirganda tengsizlik ishorasi o'zgaradi.
Chiziqli tengsizlik
\[ax + b > c \Rightarrow x > \frac{c-b}{a} \;(a>0)\]
\[ax + b > c \Rightarrow x < \frac{c-b}{a} \;(a<0)\]
Koeffitsient manfiy bo'lsa ishora o'zgaradi.
Kvadrat tengsizlik — \(ax^2+bx+c > 0\)
\[D = b^2 - 4ac,\quad x_{1,2} = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{D}}{2a}\]
\[a>0,\;D>0: \quad x < x_1 \;\text{yoki}\; x > x_2\]
\[a>0,\;D>0,\;ax^2+bx+c < 0: \quad x_1 < x < x_2\]
Eslab qoling: a>0 bo'lsa parabola yuqoriga, a<0 bo'lsa pastga ochiladi.
Modul tengsizligi
\[|f(x)| < a \Rightarrow -a < f(x) < a \quad (a>0)\]
\[|f(x)| > a \Rightarrow f(x) < -a \;\text{yoki}\; f(x) > a\]
Kasrli tengsizlik — interval usuli
\[\frac{f(x)}{g(x)} > 0\]
Usul: Surat va mahrajni nolga tenglang → son o'qida nuqtalar belgilang → ishoralarni tekshiring.

3-bosqich

Yechilgan misollar 🧮

Har bir misol ustiga bosing — bosqichma-bosqich yechimni ko'ring.

1
\(2x - 5 > 3\) tengsizligini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Doimiy sonni o'ng tomonga o'tkazing
\(2x > 3 + 5\)
2
Hisoblang
\(2x > 8\)
3
Koeffitsientga bo'ling (musbat — ishora o'zgarmaydi)
\(x > 4\)
4
Intervalni yozing
\(x \in (4, +\infty)\)
Son o'qida 4 dan o'ng tomonga.
JAVOB:\(x > 4\)
2
\(-3x + 6 \leq 0\) tengsizligini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Doimiy sonni o'tkazing
\(-3x \leq -6\)
2
Manfiy koeffitsientga bo'ling — ISHORA O'ZGARADI!
\(x \geq \frac{-6}{-3}\)
Manfiy bo'lgani uchun ≤ → ≥
3
Natija
\(x \geq 2\)
4
Interval
\(x \in [2, +\infty)\)
Kvadrat qavs — 2 ham kiradi.
JAVOB:\(x \geq 2\)
💡 Eslab qoling: Manfiy songa bo'lganda tengsizlik ishorasi ALBATTA o'zgaradi!
3
\(x^2 - x - 6 < 0\) tengsizligini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Ildizlarni topamiz: \(x^2 - x - 6 = 0\)
\(D = 1 + 24 = 25,\quad x_1 = 3,\quad x_2 = -2\)
2
a = 1 > 0, D > 0 — parabola yuqoriga ochiladi
\(ax^2+bx+c < 0 \Rightarrow x_1 < x < x_2\)
3
Javob
\(-2 < x < 3\)
Parabola pastda bo'lgan qism.
4
Interval
\(x \in (-2, 3)\)
Ochiq interval — chegaralar kirmaydi.
JAVOB:\(x \in (-2,\ 3)\)
4
\(x^2 - 4x + 4 > 0\) tengsizligini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Diskriminant
\(D = 16 - 16 = 0,\quad x_0 = 2\)
D=0 bo'lgani uchun bitta ildiz.
2
\((x-2)^2 > 0\) — bu faqat \(x = 2\) da nolga teng
\((x-2)^2 = 0 \Leftrightarrow x = 2\)
3
Boshqa hamma joyda > 0
\(x \in \mathbb{R} \setminus \{2\}\)
x=2 dan tashqari barcha sonlar.
4
Interval ko'rinishi
\(x \in (-\infty, 2) \cup (2, +\infty)\)
JAVOB:\(x \neq 2\)
💡 Farq: D=0 da tenglama bir ildizga ega, lekin tengsizlik \(>0\) bo'lsa bu nuqta chiqariladi.
5
\(|x - 3| < 5\) tengsizligini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Modul tengsizligini ochamiz: \(|f(x)| < a \Rightarrow -a < f(x) < a\)
\(-5 < x - 3 < 5\)
2
Har uchala qismga +3 qo'shamiz
\(-5 + 3 < x < 5 + 3\)
3
Natija
\(-2 < x < 8\)
4
Interval
\(x \in (-2,\ 8)\)
JAVOB:\(x \in (-2,\ 8)\)
6
\(|2x + 1| \geq 7\) tengsizligini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Modul ≥ — ikkiga ajralamiz
\(2x+1 \geq 7 \quad \text{yoki} \quad 2x+1 \leq -7\)
2
1-holat:
\(2x \geq 6 \Rightarrow x \geq 3\)
3
2-holat:
\(2x \leq -8 \Rightarrow x \leq -4\)
4
Birlashma
\(x \in (-\infty, -4] \cup [3, +\infty)\)
JAVOB:\(x \leq -4 \;\text{yoki}\; x \geq 3\)
7
\(\frac{x+2}{x-1} > 0\) tengsizligini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Surat va mahrajni nolga tenglaymiz
\(x+2=0 \Rightarrow x=-2;\quad x-1=0 \Rightarrow x=1\)
2
Son o'qida nuqtalarni belgilaymiz
\(-\infty \quad -2 \quad 1 \quad +\infty\)
3 ta soha hosil bo'ladi.
3
Har bir sohada ishorani tekshiramiz: \(x=0\) da:
\(\frac{0+2}{0-1} = \frac{2}{-1} = -2 < 0\)
O'rta soha manfiy.
4
Javob: ikki uchida musbat
\(x < -2 \quad \text{yoki} \quad x > 1\)
x=1 mahraj nolga teng — kiritilmaydi.
JAVOB:\(x \in (-\infty,\ -2) \cup (1,\ +\infty)\)
💡 Interval usuli: Nuqtalardan o'tganda ishorani almashtirib boring.
8
\(3x + 2 > x + 8\) va \(x - 1 < 4\) sistemasini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
1-tengsizlik:
\(3x - x > 8 - 2 \Rightarrow 2x > 6 \Rightarrow x > 3\)
2
2-tengsizlik:
\(x < 4 + 1 \Rightarrow x < 5\)
3
Kesishma:
\(x > 3 \quad \text{va} \quad x < 5\)
Ikkala shart bir vaqtda bajarilishi kerak.
4
Natija
\(3 < x < 5\)
JAVOB:\(x \in (3,\ 5)\)
9
\(x^2 - 5x + 6 \geq 0\) tengsizligini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Ildizlar:
\(x^2-5x+6=0 \Rightarrow x_1=2,\; x_2=3\)
2
a=1>0, \(ax^2+bx+c \geq 0\)
\(x \leq x_1 \quad \text{yoki} \quad x \geq x_2\)
Parabola yuqorida bo'lgan qism.
3
Natija
\(x \leq 2 \quad \text{yoki} \quad x \geq 3\)
4
Interval
\(x \in (-\infty, 2] \cup [3, +\infty)\)
Kvadrat qavslar — chegara nuqtalari kiradi.
JAVOB:\(x \in (-\infty,\ 2] \cup [3,\ +\infty)\)
10
\(\frac{x^2-4}{x+1} \leq 0\) tengsizligini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Surat va mahrajni faktorizatsiya:
\(x^2-4 = (x-2)(x+2),\quad x+1 \neq 0\)
2
Nol nuqtalar:
\(x = -2,\; x = -1,\; x = 2\)
3
Interval usuli — 4 ta soha tekshiramiz:
\(x < -2: \frac{(-)( -)}{(-)} = \frac{+}{-} < 0 \checkmark\)
4
Qolgan sohalar:
\(-2 0 ✗;\quad -12: + > 0 ✗\)
5
Natija (\(\leq 0\), shuning uchun ildizlar ham kiradi):
\(x \in (-\infty,-2] \cup (-1, 2]\)
x=-1 mahraj nolga teng — kiritilmaydi!
JAVOB:\(x \in (-\infty,\ -2] \cup (-1,\ 2]\)
