Ehtimollik
Probability 🎲

Atamalar → Formulalar → 10 ta yechilgan misol. Har bir qadamni tushunib o'rganing.

1-bosqich

Atamalar 📖

Probability / Ehtimollik
\(P(A)\) — hodisa ehtimoli
0 dan 1 gacha bo'lgan son. \(P = 0\) — imkonsiz, \(P = 1\) — aniq.
Sample space / Natijalar fazosi
\(\Omega\) — barcha mumkin natijalar
Masalan: tanga otganda \(\Omega = \{\text{tura, yozuv}\}\)
Event / Hodisa
\(A\) — ehtimol sanalayotgan hodisa
Masalan: zarda 6 chiqishi \(A = \{6\}\)
Favorable outcomes
Qulay natijalar
Hodisani amalga oshiradigan natijalar soni.
Classical probability
Klassik ehtimollik
\(P(A) = \frac{m}{n}\) — qulay natijalar / jami natijalar
Complement / To'ldiruvchi
\(\bar{A}\) — hodisa bo'lmasligi
\(P(\bar{A}) = 1 - P(A)\)
Independent events
Bog'liq bo'lmagan hodisalar
A hodisa B ga ta'sir qilmaydi. \(P(A \cap B) = P(A) \cdot P(B)\)
Mutually exclusive
O'zaro istisno hodisalar
Bir vaqtda sodir bo'la olmaydi. \(P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B)\)
Conditional probability
Shartli ehtimollik
\(P(A|B) = \frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(B)}\)
Bayes theorem
Bayes teoremasi
Yangi ma'lumot asosida ehtimolni yangilash.
Expected value
Kutilgan qiymat
\(E(X) = \sum x_i P(x_i)\)
Random variable
Tasodifiy miqdor
Tasodifiy natijaga bog'liq miqdor.

2-bosqich

Formulalar 📐

Klassik ehtimollik
\[P(A) = \frac{m}{n}\]
\[0 \leq P(A) \leq 1\]
\[P(\Omega) = 1, \quad P(\emptyset) = 0\]
m — qulay natijalar soni  |  n — barcha mumkin natijalar soni
Hodisalar amallari
\[P(\bar{A}) = 1 - P(A)\]
\[P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A \cap B)\]
\[P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B) \quad \text{(istisno hodisalar)}\]
Ko'paytirish qoidasi
\[P(A \cap B) = P(A) \cdot P(B) \quad \text{(bog'liq bo'lmagan)}\]
\[P(A \cap B) = P(A) \cdot P(B|A) \quad \text{(bog'liq)}\]
Kutilgan qiymat
\[E(X) = \sum_{i} x_i \cdot P(x_i)\]
Har bir natijani uning ehtimolligiga ko'paytirib yig'amiz.

3-bosqich

Yechilgan misollar 🧮

Har bir misol ustiga bosing — bosqichma-bosqich yechimni ko'ring.

1
Zarda otildi. Juft son chiqish ehtimolini toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Juft sonlar:
\(A = \{2, 4, 6\},\quad m = 3\)
2
Jami natijalar:
\(n = 6\)
3
Ehtimollik:
\(P(A) = \frac{3}{6} = \frac{1}{2}\)
JAVOB:\(P = 0.5\)
2
Tanga ikki marta otildi. Ikkalasi ham tura chiqish ehtimoli?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Har bir otishda:
\(P(\text{tura}) = \frac{1}{2}\)
2
Bog'liq bo'lmagan:
\(P = \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{1}{2} = \frac{1}{4}\)
JAVOB:\(P = \dfrac{1}{4} = 0.25\)
3
Qopda 3 qizil, 5 ko'k sharcha bor. Bitta olib, qizil bo'lmash ehtimoli?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Ko'k sharcha ehtimoli:
\(P(\text{ko'k}) = \frac{5}{8}\)
2
Yoki to'ldiruvchi:
\(P(\bar{A}) = 1 - P(\text{qizil}) = 1 - \frac{3}{8} = \frac{5}{8}\)
JAVOB:\(P = \dfrac{5}{8}\)
4
Bir zardada 3 dan katta YOKI juft son chiqish ehtimoli?
👁 Ko'rish
1
3 dan katta: \(A = \{4,5,6\}\), \(P(A) = 3/6\)
\(P(A) = \frac{1}{2}\)
2
Juft: \(B = \{2,4,6\}\), \(P(B) = 3/6\)
\(P(B) = \frac{1}{2}\)
3
Kesishma \(A \cap B = \{4,6\}\)
\(P(A \cap B) = \frac{2}{6} = \frac{1}{3}\)
4
Birlashma:
\(P(A \cup B) = \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{3} = \frac{2}{3}\)
JAVOB:\(P = \dfrac{2}{3}\)
5
10 ta kartada 1-10 sonlar. Juft va 5 dan katta bo'lgan son chiqishi?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Juft va 5 dan katta: \(\{6, 8, 10\}\)
\(m = 3,\quad n = 10\)
2
Ehtimollik:
\(P = \frac{3}{10}\)
JAVOB:\(P = 0.3\)
6
Sinf: 15 qiz, 10 o'g'il. 2 ta o'quvchi tanlandi. Ikkalasi qiz bo'lish ehtimoli?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Birinchi qiz:
\(P_1 = \frac{15}{25}\)
2
Ikkinchi qiz (birinchi tanlanganidan keyin):
\(P_2 = \frac{14}{24}\)
3
Ko'paytirish:
\(P = \frac{15}{25} \times \frac{14}{24} = \frac{210}{600} = \frac{7}{20}\)
JAVOB:\(P = \dfrac{7}{20} = 0.35\)
7
Zarda 3 marta otildi. Hech bo'lmasa bir marta 6 chiqishi ehtimoli?
👁 Ko'rish
1
To'ldiruvchidan: '6 hech chiqmasin'
\(P(\text{6 chiqmasin}) = \frac{5}{6}\)
2
3 marta hammasi 6 chiqmasin:
\(P(\bar{A}) = \left(\frac{5}{6}\right)^3 = \frac{125}{216}\)
3
Hech bo'lmasa bir marta:
\(P(A) = 1 - \frac{125}{216} = \frac{91}{216} \approx 0.42\)
JAVOB:\(P \approx 0.42\)
8
Kutilgan qiymatni hisoblang: X = 1 (P=0.3), X = 2 (P=0.5), X = 5 (P=0.2).
👁 Ko'rish
1
Formuladan:
\(E(X) = 1(0.3) + 2(0.5) + 5(0.2)\)
2
Hisob:
\(= 0.3 + 1.0 + 1.0 = 2.3\)
JAVOB:\(E(X) = 2.3\)
9
\(P(A) = 0.4,\; P(B) = 0.3\). A va B bog'liq bo'lmasa, \(P(A \cup B)\) ni toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Ko'paytirish:
\(P(A \cap B) = 0.4 \times 0.3 = 0.12\)
2
Birlashma:
\(P(A \cup B) = 0.4 + 0.3 - 0.12 = 0.58\)
JAVOB:\(P(A \cup B) = 0.58\)
10
Paketda 4 ta yaxshi, 2 ta yomon mahsulot. 2 ta olinsa, ikkalasi yaxshi bo'lish ehtimoli?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Birinchi yaxshi:
\(P_1 = \frac{4}{6} = \frac{2}{3}\)
2
Ikkinchi yaxshi:
\(P_2 = \frac{3}{5}\)
Birinchi olinganidan keyin 3 ta yaxshi qoladi.
3
Ko'paytirish:
\(P = \frac{2}{3} \times \frac{3}{5} = \frac{6}{15} = \frac{2}{5}\)
JAVOB:\(P = \dfrac{2}{5} = 0.4\)
