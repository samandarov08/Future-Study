Stereometriya
Solid Geometry 🧊

Atamalar → Formulalar → 10 ta yechilgan misol. Har bir qadamni tushunib o'rganing.

1-bosqich

Atamalar 📖

Solid / Jism
3 o'lchamli geometrik shakl
Uzunlik, kenglik va balandlikka ega.
Volume / Hajm
Jism egallagan fazo
Kub birliklarda: cm³, m³
Surface area
To'liq yuza
Jismning tashqi yuzasi yig'indisi.
Lateral surface
Yon yuza
Asoslar tashqaridagi yuzalar.
Cube / Kub
Barcha tomonlari teng to'g'ri burchakli parallelepiped
\(V = a^3,\; S = 6a^2\)
Rectangular prism
To'g'ri burchakli parallelepiped
\(V = l \times w \times h\)
Sphere / Shar
Markazdan teng masofadagi nuqtalar to'plami
\(V = \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3,\; S = 4\pi r^2\)
Cylinder / Silindr
Ikki parallel aylana va yon yuzadan iborat
\(V = \pi r^2 h\)
Cone / Konus
Aylana asos va bir tepa nuqtasi
\(V = \frac{1}{3}\pi r^2 h\)
Pyramid / Piramida
Ko'pburchak asos va uchburchak yon yuzalar
\(V = \frac{1}{3}Sh\)
Diagonal / Diagonal
Parallelepiped ichidagi diagonal
\(d = \sqrt{l^2 + w^2 + h^2}\)
Cross section
Ko'ndalang kesim
Jismni tekislik bilan kesish natijasida hosil bo'lgan shakl.

2-bosqich

Formulalar 📐

Prizma va parallelepiped
\[V = S_{\text{asos}} \times h\]
\[\text{To'g'ri burchakli: } V = lwh,\; S_{\text{to'liq}} = 2(lw + lh + wh)\]
\[\text{Kub: } V = a^3,\; S = 6a^2,\; d = a\sqrt{3}\]
Piramida va konus
\[V_{\text{piramida}} = \frac{1}{3} S_{\text{asos}} \cdot h\]
\[V_{\text{konus}} = \frac{1}{3} \pi r^2 h\]
\[S_{\text{konus yon}} = \pi r l, \quad l = \sqrt{r^2 + h^2} \text{ (apofema)}\]
Shar va silindr
\[V_{\text{shar}} = \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3, \quad S_{\text{shar}} = 4\pi r^2\]
\[V_{\text{silindr}} = \pi r^2 h\]
\[S_{\text{silindr to'liq}} = 2\pi r^2 + 2\pi rh = 2\pi r(r + h)\]
Asosiy munosabat
\[V_{\text{piramida}} = \frac{1}{3} V_{\text{prizma}} \quad \text{(bir xil asos va balandlikda)}\]
\[V_{\text{konus}} = \frac{1}{3} V_{\text{silindr}} \quad \text{(bir xil r va h da)}\]

3-bosqich

Yechilgan misollar 🧮

Har bir misol ustiga bosing — bosqichma-bosqich yechimni ko'ring.

1
Kub: \(a = 4\) sm. Hajm va to'liq yuzani toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Hajm:
\(V = a^3 = 4^3 = 64 \text{ sm}^3\)
2
Yuza:
\(S = 6a^2 = 6 \times 16 = 96 \text{ sm}^2\)
JAVOB:\(V = 64 \text{ sm}^3,\quad S = 96 \text{ sm}^2\)
2
To'g'ri burchakli parallelepiped: \(l=5, w=3, h=4\). Hajm va diagonal?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Hajm:
\(V = 5 \times 3 \times 4 = 60 \text{ sm}^3\)
2
Diagonal:
\(d = \sqrt{5^2 + 3^2 + 4^2} = \sqrt{25+9+16} = \sqrt{50} = 5\sqrt{2}\)
JAVOB:\(V = 60,\quad d = 5\sqrt{2}\)
3
Silindr: \(r = 3\) sm, \(h = 10\) sm. Hajm va to'liq yuza?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Hajm:
\(V = \pi r^2 h = \pi \times 9 \times 10 = 90\pi \approx 283 \text{ sm}^3\)
2
Yuza:
\(S = 2\pi r(r+h) = 2\pi \times 3 \times 13 = 78\pi \approx 245 \text{ sm}^2\)
JAVOB:\(V = 90\pi,\quad S = 78\pi\)
4
Konus: \(r = 4\) sm, \(h = 3\) sm. Hajm va yon yuzani toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Apofema:
\(l = \sqrt{r^2 + h^2} = \sqrt{16+9} = 5\)
2
Hajm:
\(V = \frac{1}{3}\pi r^2 h = \frac{1}{3}\pi \times 16 \times 3 = 16\pi\)
3
Yon yuza:
\(S_{\text{yon}} = \pi r l = \pi \times 4 \times 5 = 20\pi\)
JAVOB:\(V = 16\pi,\quad S_{\text{yon}} = 20\pi\)
5
Shar: \(r = 6\) sm. Hajm va yuzani toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Hajm:
\(V = \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3 = \frac{4}{3}\pi \times 216 = 288\pi \approx 905 \text{ sm}^3\)
2
Yuza:
\(S = 4\pi r^2 = 4\pi \times 36 = 144\pi \approx 452 \text{ sm}^2\)
JAVOB:\(V = 288\pi,\quad S = 144\pi\)
6
To'g'ri to'rtburchakli piramida: asos \(6 \times 6\), balandlik \(h = 4\). Hajm?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Asos yuzasi:
\(S_{\text{asos}} = 6 \times 6 = 36 \text{ sm}^2\)
2
Hajm:
\(V = \frac{1}{3} \times 36 \times 4 = 48 \text{ sm}^3\)
JAVOB:\(V = 48 \text{ sm}^3\)
7
Silindr hajmi 200\(\pi\) sm³, \(r = 5\). Balandlik \(h\)?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Formuladan:
\(\pi r^2 h = 200\pi\)
2
\(25h = 200 \Rightarrow h = 8 \text{ sm}\)
JAVOB:\(h = 8 \text{ sm}\)
8
Shar diametri \(d = 10\) sm. Hajmi?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Radius:
\(r = d/2 = 5 \text{ sm}\)
2
Hajm:
\(V = \frac{4}{3}\pi \times 125 = \frac{500\pi}{3} \approx 524 \text{ sm}^3\)
JAVOB:\(V = \dfrac{500\pi}{3} \approx 524 \text{ sm}^3\)
9
Bir xil asos va balandlikdagi silindr va konus hajmlari nisbati?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Silindr:
\(V_{\text{sil}} = \pi r^2 h\)
2
Konus:
\(V_{\text{kon}} = \frac{1}{3}\pi r^2 h\)
3
Nisbat:
\(\frac{V_{\text{sil}}}{V_{\text{kon}}} = \frac{\pi r^2 h}{\frac{1}{3}\pi r^2 h} = 3\)
JAVOB:Silindr : Konus = \(3 : 1\)
10
Kubning hajmi \(216\) sm³. Uning yuzasi va diagonali?
👁 Ko'rish
1
Tomon:
\(a = \sqrt[3]{216} = 6 \text{ sm}\)
2
Yuza:
\(S = 6 \times 36 = 216 \text{ sm}^2\)
3
Diagonal:
\(d = 6\sqrt{3} \approx 10.39 \text{ sm}\)
JAVOB:\(S = 216 \text{ sm}^2,\quad d = 6\sqrt{3}\)
