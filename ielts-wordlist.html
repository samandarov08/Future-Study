Ta'lim / Education

10 ta so'z
compulsory adj
majburiy
Education is compulsory for children aged 6 to 16 in most countries.
curriculum noun
o'quv dasturi
The school's curriculum includes science, mathematics and foreign languages.
literacy noun
savodxonlik
Improving literacy rates is essential for economic development.
scholarship noun
stipendiya, grant
She received a full scholarship to study at Oxford University.
academic adj
akademik, o'quv
His academic performance improved significantly after he changed his study habits.
tuition noun
o'qish haqi; o'qitish
University tuition fees have risen dramatically over the past decade.
graduate verb/noun
bitiruvchi; bitirmoq
She plans to graduate from university next year with a degree in engineering.
critical thinking phrase
tanqidiy fikrlash
Critical thinking skills are highly valued by employers in all industries.
vocational adj
kasbiy, amaliy
Vocational training programmes help young people develop practical workplace skills.
assessment noun
baholash
Continuous assessment is often considered fairer than a single final examination.

Jamiyat / Society

10 ta so'z
inequality noun
tengsizlik
Income inequality has grown significantly in many developed nations.
diversity noun
xilma-xillik
Cultural diversity enriches society and promotes creativity and innovation.
welfare noun
ijtimoiy ta'minot; farovonlik
The government increased spending on child welfare programmes.
integration noun
integratsiya, qo'shilish
The integration of immigrants into local communities requires time and support.
prejudice noun
tarafkashlik, oldindan fikr
Racial prejudice continues to be a serious problem in many parts of the world.
cohesion noun
birlik, hamjihatlik
Social cohesion is essential for a stable and peaceful community.
urbanisation noun
shaharlanish jarayoni
Rapid urbanisation has led to overcrowding in many major cities.
demographic adj/noun
demografik; aholi qatlami
The country faces significant challenges due to an ageing demographic.
marginalised adj
chetga surilgan, e'tibordan chetda
The policy aims to improve conditions for marginalised communities.
stereotype noun
stereotip, qolip fikr
It is important to challenge harmful stereotypes about different groups of people.

Fan va Texnologiya / Science & Technology

10 ta so'z
innovation noun
innovatsiya, yangilik
Technological innovation has transformed the way we communicate and work.
breakthrough noun
muhim kashfiyot, yutuq
Scientists achieved a major breakthrough in cancer research last year.
automation noun
avtomatlashtirish
Automation is expected to replace many routine jobs in the coming decades.
algorithm noun
algoritm
Social media platforms use complex algorithms to determine what content users see.
empirical adj
empirik, tajribaga asoslangan
The study provides empirical evidence that exercise improves mental health.
hypothesis noun
gipoteza, taxmin
The scientist's hypothesis was confirmed by several independent experiments.
surveillance noun
kuzatuv, nazorat
The increasing use of surveillance cameras raises serious privacy concerns.
renewable adj
qayta tiklanadigan
Investing in renewable energy sources is crucial for combating climate change.
artificial intelligence phrase
sun'iy intellekt
Artificial intelligence is transforming industries from healthcare to finance.
transparent adj
shaffof, ochiq
Governments must be more transparent about how they use citizens' data.

Iqtisodiyot / Economy

10 ta so'z
globalisation noun
globallashuv
Globalisation has created new opportunities for trade but also increased competition.
inflation noun
inflyatsiya
Rising inflation has reduced the purchasing power of ordinary households.
sustainable adj
barqaror, uzoq muddatli
We need sustainable economic growth that does not damage the environment.
entrepreneurship noun
tadbirkorlik
Entrepreneurship drives economic growth and creates employment opportunities.
subsidy noun
subsidiya, dotatsiya
The government provides subsidies to farmers to keep food prices affordable.
expenditure noun
xarajat, sarf
Government expenditure on healthcare has increased significantly over the past decade.
commodity noun
tovar, xomashyo
Oil is the world's most traded commodity and affects global economic stability.
recession noun
iqtisodiy tanazzul
The country entered a severe recession following the financial crisis.
monopoly noun
monopoliya
The company holds a near-complete monopoly over the domestic market.
fluctuate verb
o'zgarib turmoq, tebranmoq
Oil prices tend to fluctuate depending on global supply and demand.

Muhit / Environment

10 ta so'z

Carbon emissions from transport account for a significant share of global warming.

o'rmonlarni yo'q qilish
Deforestation in the Amazon is destroying biodiversity at an alarming rate.
biodiversity noun
biologik xilma-xillik
Protecting biodiversity is essential for maintaining healthy ecosystems.
contamination noun
ifloslanish, zaharlanish
Water contamination from industrial waste poses serious health risks.
ecosystem noun
ekotizim
Coral reefs are among the most diverse ecosystems on the planet.
drought noun
qurg'oqchilik
Prolonged drought has devastated crops and threatened food supplies in the region.
mitigation noun
yumshatish, kamaytirish
Climate change mitigation requires urgent action from governments worldwide.
conservation noun
muhofaza qilish, saqlash
Wildlife conservation efforts have helped several endangered species recover.
fossil fuels phrase
qazilma yoqilg'i
Burning fossil fuels is the leading cause of greenhouse gas emissions.
extinction noun
yo'q bo'lib ketish
Many scientists warn that we are facing a sixth mass extinction event.
