Trigonometriya
Trigonometry 📐

Atamalar → Formulalar → 10 ta yechilgan misol. Har bir qadamni tushunib o'rganing.

1-bosqich

Atamalar 📖

Sine / Sinus
\(\sin\alpha\)
To'g'ri burchakli uchburchakda: \(\sin\alpha = \frac{\text{qarshi katet}}{\text{gipotenuza}}\)
Cosine / Kosinus
\(\cos\alpha\)
\(\cos\alpha = \frac{\text{yonma-yon katet}}{\text{gipotenuza}}\)
Tangent / Tangens
\(\tan\alpha\)
\(\tan\alpha = \frac{\sin\alpha}{\cos\alpha} = \frac{\text{qarshi katet}}{\text{yonma-yon katet}}\)
Cotangent / Kotangent
\(\cot\alpha\)
\(\cot\alpha = \frac{\cos\alpha}{\sin\alpha}\)
Unit circle
Birlik aylana
Markazi (0,0) da, radiusi 1 bo'lgan aylana. \((\cos\alpha, \sin\alpha)\) — nuqta koordinatasi.
Radian
Radian — burchak o'lchovi
\(180° = \pi \text{ radian}\), \(1° = \frac{\pi}{180}\)
Quadrant
Chorak
Koordinata tekisligining to'rt bo'lagi. I, II, III, IV choraks.
Reference angle
Asosiy burchak
Har qanday burchakni birinchi chorakdagi o'tkir burchakka keltirish.
Pythagorean identity
Pifagor tenglamasi
\(\sin^2\alpha + \cos^2\alpha = 1\)
Period / Davr
Funksiyaning takrorlanish davri
\(\sin\) va \(\cos\) uchun davr \(2\pi\), \(\tan\) uchun \(\pi\).
Amplitude
Amplituda
\(y = A\sin(x)\) da \(A\) — eng katta qiymat.
Phase shift
Fazaviy siljish
\(y = \sin(x - c)\) da \(c\) — grafik gorizontal siljishi.

2-bosqich

Formulalar 📐

Asosiy qiymatlar
\[\sin 0°=0,\;\sin 30°=\tfrac{1}{2},\;\sin 45°=\tfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2},\;\sin 60°=\tfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2},\;\sin 90°=1\]
\[\cos 0°=1,\;\cos 30°=\tfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2},\;\cos 45°=\tfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2},\;\cos 60°=\tfrac{1}{2},\;\cos 90°=0\]
\[\tan 0°=0,\;\tan 30°=\tfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}},\;\tan 45°=1,\;\tan 60°=\sqrt{3}\]
Bu qiymatlarni yodlash shart — barcha masalalarda kerak bo'ladi!
Asosiy tengliklar
\[\sin^2\alpha + \cos^2\alpha = 1\]
\[1 + \tan^2\alpha = \frac{1}{\cos^2\alpha}\]
\[1 + \cot^2\alpha = \frac{1}{\sin^2\alpha}\]
Qo'shish formulalari
\[\sin(\alpha \pm \beta) = \sin\alpha\cos\beta \pm \cos\alpha\sin\beta\]
\[\cos(\alpha \pm \beta) = \cos\alpha\cos\beta \mp \sin\alpha\sin\beta\]
\[\tan(\alpha + \beta) = \frac{\tan\alpha + \tan\beta}{1 - \tan\alpha\tan\beta}\]
Ikkilangan burchak
\[\sin 2\alpha = 2\sin\alpha\cos\alpha\]
\[\cos 2\alpha = \cos^2\alpha - \sin^2\alpha = 1 - 2\sin^2\alpha = 2\cos^2\alpha - 1\]

3-bosqich

Yechilgan misollar 🧮

Har bir misol ustiga bosing — bosqichma-bosqich yechimni ko'ring.

1
\(\sin^2 30° + \cos^2 30°\) ni hisoblang.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Qiymatlarni qo'yamiz:
\(\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\right)^2\)
2
Hisob:
\(\frac{1}{4} + \frac{3}{4} = 1\)
Pifagor tenglamasi tasdiqlandi!
JAVOB:\(1\)
2
\(\tan 45° + \sin 90°\) ni hisoblang.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Qiymatlardan:
\(\tan 45° = 1,\quad \sin 90° = 1\)
2
Natija:
\(1 + 1 = 2\)
JAVOB:\(2\)
3
\(\sin 60° \cdot \cos 30° - \cos 60° \cdot \sin 30°\) ni hisoblang.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Bu \(\sin(60° - 30°)\) formulasi:
\(= \sin 30° = \frac{1}{2}\)
Qo'shish formulasini tanidingizmi?
2
To'g'ridan hisoblash:
\(\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\cdot\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} - \frac{1}{2}\cdot\frac{1}{2} = \frac{3}{4} - \frac{1}{4} = \frac{1}{2}\checkmark\)
JAVOB:\(\dfrac{1}{2}\)
4
\(\sin\alpha = \frac{3}{5}\) va \(\alpha\) birinchi chorakda. \(\cos\alpha\) ni toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Pifagor tenglamasidan:
\(\cos^2\alpha = 1 - \sin^2\alpha = 1 - \frac{9}{25} = \frac{16}{25}\)
2
I chorakda cos musbat:
\(\cos\alpha = \frac{4}{5}\)
JAVOB:\(\cos\alpha = \dfrac{4}{5}\)
5
\(\sin 2\alpha\) ni toping, agar \(\sin\alpha = \frac{1}{2}\) va \(\cos\alpha = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\).
👁 Ko'rish
1
Ikkilangan burchak formulasi:
\(\sin 2\alpha = 2\sin\alpha\cos\alpha\)
2
Hisob:
\(= 2 \cdot \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\)
Bu aslida \(\sin 60°\).
JAVOB:\(\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\)
6
\(\cos^2\alpha - \sin^2\alpha\) ifodani soddalshtiring.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Ikkilangan burchak formulasi:
\(\cos^2\alpha - \sin^2\alpha = \cos 2\alpha\)
JAVOB:\(\cos 2\alpha\)
7
\(\tan\alpha = \sqrt{3}\) bo'lsa, \(\alpha\) ni toping (\(0° \leq \alpha \leq 360°\)).
👁 Ko'rish
1
\(\tan 60° = \sqrt{3}\)
\(\alpha_1 = 60°\)
I chorakda.
2
\(\tan\) III chorakda ham musbat:
\(\alpha_2 = 180° + 60° = 240°\)
JAVOB:\(\alpha = 60°\text{ yoki }240°\)
8
\(\sin(45° + 30°)\) ni hisoblang.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Qo'shish formulasi:
\(\sin 45°\cos 30° + \cos 45°\sin 30°\)
2
Qiymatlarni qo'yamiz:
\(\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\cdot\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} + \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\cdot\frac{1}{2}\)
3
Natija:
\(\frac{\sqrt{6}}{4} + \frac{\sqrt{2}}{4} = \frac{\sqrt{6}+\sqrt{2}}{4}\)
JAVOB:\(\dfrac{\sqrt{6}+\sqrt{2}}{4}\)
9
\(2\sin^2\alpha + 3\cos^2\alpha = 2.5\) tenglamasini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
\(\cos^2\alpha = 1 - \sin^2\alpha\) ni qo'yamiz:
\(2\sin^2\alpha + 3(1 - \sin^2\alpha) = 2.5\)
2
Ochib yozamiz:
\(2\sin^2\alpha + 3 - 3\sin^2\alpha = 2.5\)
3
\(-\sin^2\alpha = -0.5 \Rightarrow \sin^2\alpha = 0.5\)
4
Natija:
\(\sin\alpha = \pm\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2} \Rightarrow \alpha = 45°, 135°, 225°, 315°\)
JAVOB:\(\alpha = 45°,\;135°,\;225°,\;315°\)
10
\(\frac{1 - \cos 2\alpha}{\sin 2\alpha}\) ifodani soddalashtiring.
👁 Ko'rish
1
\(1 - \cos 2\alpha = 2\sin^2\alpha\):
\(\frac{2\sin^2\alpha}{\sin 2\alpha}\)
2
\(\sin 2\alpha = 2\sin\alpha\cos\alpha\):
\(\frac{2\sin^2\alpha}{2\sin\alpha\cos\alpha}\)
3
Qisqartiring:
\(\frac{\sin\alpha}{\cos\alpha} = \tan\alpha\)
JAVOB:\(\tan\alpha\)
