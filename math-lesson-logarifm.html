Logarifm
Logarithms 🔬

Atamalar → Formulalar → 10 ta yechilgan misol. Har bir qadamni tushunib o'rganing.

1-bosqich

Atamalar 📖

Logarithm / Logarifm
\(\log_a b = c \Leftrightarrow a^c = b\)
Logarifm — darajani topuvchi amal. Masalan: \(\log_2 8 = 3\) chunki \(2^3 = 8\)
Base / Asos
\(a\) — logarifm asosi
\(\log_a b\) da \(a\) — asos. \(a > 0,\; a \neq 1\)
Argument
\(b\) — logarifm argumenti
\(\log_a b\) da \(b > 0\) bo'lishi shart.
Common logarithm
\(\log_{10} = \lg\)
Asosi 10 bo'lgan logarifm. Kalkulyatorda 'log' tugmasi.
Natural logarithm
\(\ln = \log_e\)
Asosi \(e \approx 2.718\) bo'lgan logarifm. Kalkulyatorda 'ln' tugmasi.
Antilogarithm
Logarifmga teskari amal
\(\log_a x = b \Rightarrow x = a^b\)
Logarithmic identity
\(\log_a a = 1\)
Har qanday son o'ziga o'zi logarifmga 1 teng.
Zero logarithm
\(\log_a 1 = 0\)
Istalgan asosda 1 ning logarifmi 0.
Change of base
Asosni almashtirish
\(\log_a b = \frac{\ln b}{\ln a} = \frac{\log b}{\log a}\)
Logarithmic equation
Logarifmik tenglama
Noma'lum logarifm belgisi ichida bo'lgan tenglama.
Exponential function
Eksponensial funksiya
\(y = a^x\) — logarifmning teskari funksiyasi.
Domain of logarithm
Aniqlanish sohasi
\(\log_a f(x)\) uchun \(f(x) > 0\) bo'lishi shart.

2-bosqich

Formulalar 📐

Asosiy ta'rif
\[\log_a b = c \Leftrightarrow a^c = b\]
\[\log_a a = 1, \quad \log_a 1 = 0, \quad \log_a a^n = n\]
Logarifm xossalari
\[\log_a(mn) = \log_a m + \log_a n\]
\[\log_a\frac{m}{n} = \log_a m - \log_a n\]
\[\log_a m^n = n \cdot \log_a m\]
\[\log_a b = \frac{1}{\log_b a}\]
Asosni almashtirish
\[\log_a b = \frac{\log_c b}{\log_c a} = \frac{\ln b}{\ln a}\]
Kalkulyatorda har qanday asosni hisoblash uchun ishlatiladi.
Logarifmik tenglama va tengsizlik
\[\log_a f(x) = \log_a g(x) \Rightarrow f(x) = g(x) \quad (f(x)>0, g(x)>0)\]
\[a > 1: \log_a f > \log_a g \Leftrightarrow f > g\]
\[0 < a < 1: \log_a f > \log_a g \Leftrightarrow f < g\]
Diqqat: 0 < a < 1 bo'lsa tengsizlik ishorasi o'zgaradi!

3-bosqich

Yechilgan misollar 🧮

Har bir misol ustiga bosing — bosqichma-bosqich yechimni ko'ring.

1
\(\log_2 32\) ni hisoblang.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Ta'rifdan: \(2^? = 32\)
\(2^5 = 32\)
2
Javob:
\(\log_2 32 = 5\)
JAVOB:\(5\)
2
\(\log_3 \frac{1}{27}\) ni hisoblang.
👁 Ko'rish
1
\(3^? = \frac{1}{27} = 3^{-3}\)
\(\log_3 3^{-3} = -3\)
Manfiy daraja ham bo'lishi mumkin.
JAVOB:\(-3\)
3
\(\log_2 8 + \log_2 4\) ni hisoblang.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Ko'paytma xossasi:
\(\log_2(8 \times 4) = \log_2 32\)
2
Hisob:
\(\log_2 32 = 5\)
JAVOB:\(5\)
4
\(\log_3 18 - \log_3 2\) ni hisoblang.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Bo'linma xossasi:
\(\log_3 \frac{18}{2} = \log_3 9\)
2
Hisob:
\(\log_3 9 = \log_3 3^2 = 2\)
JAVOB:\(2\)
5
\(\log_5 125^3\) ni hisoblang.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Daraja xossasi:
\(3 \cdot \log_5 125\)
2
\(\log_5 125 = \log_5 5^3 = 3\)
\(3 \cdot 3 = 9\)
JAVOB:\(9\)
6
\(\log_2 x = 5\) tenglamasini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Ta'rifdan:
\(x = 2^5\)
2
Hisob:
\(x = 32\)
JAVOB:\(x = 32\)
7
\(\log_3(x + 1) = \log_3(2x - 5)\) tenglamasini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Asoslar teng, shuning uchun:
\(x + 1 = 2x - 5\)
2
Yechamiz:
\(6 = x\)
3
Tekshirish (x > 0 shart):
\(\log_3 7 = \log_3 7 \checkmark\)
JAVOB:\(x = 6\)
8
\(\log_2(x^2 - 5x + 6) = 1\) tenglamasini yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
\(\log_2 f(x) = 1 \Rightarrow f(x) = 2^1 = 2\)
\(x^2 - 5x + 6 = 2\)
2
\(x^2 - 5x + 4 = 0\)
3
Ildizlar:
\((x-1)(x-4) = 0 \Rightarrow x = 1 \text{ yoki } x = 4\)
4
Tekshirish (argument > 0):
\(x=1: 1-5+6=2>0\checkmark;\quad x=4: 16-20+6=2>0\checkmark\)
JAVOB:\(x = 1 \text{ yoki } x = 4\)
9
\(\log_{10} 50 + \log_{10} 2\) ni hisoblang.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Ko'paytma xossasi:
\(\log_{10}(50 \times 2) = \log_{10} 100\)
2
Hisob:
\(\log_{10} 100 = \log_{10} 10^2 = 2\)
JAVOB:\(2\)
10
\(\log_4 x = \frac{3}{2}\) ni yeching.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Ta'rifdan:
\(x = 4^{3/2} = (\sqrt{4})^3\)
2
Hisob:
\(x = 2^3 = 8\)
JAVOB:\(x = 8\)
