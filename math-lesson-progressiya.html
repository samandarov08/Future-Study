1-bosqich

Atamalar 📖

Arithmetic progression (AP)
Arifmetik progressiya
Har bir keyingi had oldingi haddan bir xil son bilan farq qiladi: \(2, 5, 8, 11, ...\)
Common difference / Farq
\(d = a_{n+1} - a_n\)
AP da ketma-ket hadlar farqi. Masalan: \(d = 5 - 2 = 3\)
Geometric progression (GP)
Geometrik progressiya
Har bir keyingi had oldingi hadga bir xil son ko'paytiriladi: \(2, 6, 18, 54, ...\)
Common ratio / Kvazient
\(q = \frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\)
GP da ketma-ket hadlar nisbati. Masalan: \(q = 6/2 = 3\)
First term / Birinchi had
\(a_1\)
Progressiyaning birinchi elementi.
n-th term / n-had
\(a_n\)
Progressiyaning n-chi elementi.
Sum of n terms
n had yig'indisi
\(S_n\) — birinchi n ta hadning yig'indisi.
Infinite geometric series
Cheksiz GP yig'indisi
\(|q| < 1\) bo'lsa yig'indi mavjud: \(S = \frac{a_1}{1-q}\)
Arithmetic mean
Arifmetik o'rta
\(\frac{a_{n-1} + a_{n+1}}{2} = a_n\) — qo'shni hadlar o'rtasi.
Geometric mean
Geometrik o'rta
\(\sqrt{a_{n-1} \cdot a_{n+1}} = a_n\) — qo'shni hadlar geometric o'rtasi.
Monotone progression
Monoton progressiya
d>0 bo'lsa o'suvchi, d<0 bo'lsa kamayuvchi AP.
Telescoping sum
Teleskopik yig'indi
Hadlar birining ikkinchisini qisqartiradigan yig'indi.

2-bosqich

Formulalar 📐

Arifmetik progressiya (AP)
\[a_n = a_1 + (n-1)d\]
\[S_n = \frac{n(a_1 + a_n)}{2} = \frac{n(2a_1 + (n-1)d)}{2}\]
a₁ — birinchi had  |  d — farq  |  n — hadlar soni
Geometrik progressiya (GP)
\[a_n = a_1 \cdot q^{n-1}\]
\[S_n = \frac{a_1(q^n - 1)}{q - 1} \quad (q \neq 1)\]
\[S_n = n \cdot a_1 \quad (q = 1)\]
a₁ — birinchi had  |  q — kvazient
Cheksiz GP yig'indisi
\[S = \frac{a_1}{1 - q} \quad (|q| < 1)\]
Faqat \(|q| < 1\) bo'lganida cheksiz GP yig'indisi mavjud.
Arifmetik va Geometrik o'rta
\[\text{Arifmetik o'rta: } A = \frac{a + b}{2}\]
\[\text{Geometrik o'rta: } G = \sqrt{ab}\]
\[A \geq G \quad (a, b > 0)\]

3-bosqich

Yechilgan misollar 🧮

Har bir misol ustiga bosing — bosqichma-bosqich yechimni ko'ring.

1
AP: \(a_1 = 3,\; d = 4\). \(a_{10}\) ni toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Formuladan:
\(a_{10} = a_1 + (10-1)d = 3 + 9 \cdot 4\)
2
Hisob
\(a_{10} = 3 + 36 = 39\)
JAVOB:\(a_{10} = 39\)
2
AP birinchi 20 ta hadining yig'indisini toping: \(a_1 = 5,\; d = 3\).
👁 Ko'rish
1
a₂₀ ni topamiz
\(a_{20} = 5 + 19 \cdot 3 = 62\)
2
Yig'indi formulasi
\(S_{20} = \frac{20(5 + 62)}{2} = \frac{20 \cdot 67}{2}\)
3
Hisob
\(S_{20} = 10 \cdot 67 = 670\)
JAVOB:\(S_{20} = 670\)
3
AP: \(a_3 = 11,\; a_7 = 27\). \(a_1\) va \(d\) ni toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Sistemani tuzamiz
\(a_3 = a_1 + 2d = 11\)
2
\(a_7 = a_1 + 6d = 27\)
3
Ayiramiz: 2-dan 1-
\(4d = 16 \Rightarrow d = 4\)
4
a₁ ni topamiz
\(a_1 = 11 - 2 \cdot 4 = 3\)
JAVOB:\(a_1 = 3,\; d = 4\)
4
GP: \(a_1 = 2,\; q = 3\). \(a_6\) ni toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Formuladan:
\(a_6 = a_1 \cdot q^5 = 2 \cdot 3^5\)
2
Hisob
\(3^5 = 243\)
3
Natija
\(a_6 = 2 \cdot 243 = 486\)
JAVOB:\(a_6 = 486\)
5
GP birinchi 5 ta hadining yig'indisi: \(a_1 = 4,\; q = 2\).
👁 Ko'rish
1
Formuladan:
\(S_5 = \frac{4(2^5 - 1)}{2 - 1} = \frac{4 \cdot 31}{1}\)
2
Hisob
\(S_5 = 4 \cdot 31 = 124\)
JAVOB:\(S_5 = 124\)
6
Cheksiz GP: \(a_1 = 6,\; q = \frac{1}{3}\). \(S\) ni toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Shart tekshirish
\(|q| = \frac{1}{3} < 1 \checkmark\)
Cheksiz yig'indi mavjud.
2
Formuladan:
\(S = \frac{a_1}{1-q} = \frac{6}{1 - \frac{1}{3}} = \frac{6}{\frac{2}{3}}\)
3
Hisob
\(S = 6 \cdot \frac{3}{2} = 9\)
JAVOB:\(S = 9\)
7
AP: \(a_5 = 20,\; S_5 = 70\). \(a_1\) ni toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
S₅ formulasi
\(S_5 = \frac{5(a_1 + a_5)}{2} = 70\)
2
Yechamiz
\(a_1 + 20 = \frac{70 \cdot 2}{5} = 28\)
3
Natija
\(a_1 = 28 - 20 = 8\)
JAVOB:\(a_1 = 8\)
8
3 ta son AP ni hosil qiladi va ularning yig'indisi 15. O'rtacha son nima?
👁 Ko'rish
1
AP da o'rta had = arifmetik o'rta
\(\frac{a_1 + a_2 + a_3}{3} = \frac{15}{3} = 5\)
2
AP da o'rta had
\(a_2 = \frac{a_1 + a_3}{2}\)
3
Xulosa
\(a_2 = 5\)
O'rta son 5 ga teng.
JAVOB:\(a_2 = 5\)
9
GP: \(a_2 = 6,\; a_5 = 162\). \(q\) ni toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Formuladan:
\(a_5 = a_2 \cdot q^3 \Rightarrow 162 = 6 \cdot q^3\)
2
\(q^3 = 27 \Rightarrow q = 3\)
3
a₁ ni topamiz
\(a_1 = \frac{a_2}{q} = \frac{6}{3} = 2\)
JAVOB:\(q = 3,\; a_1 = 2\)
10
\(1 + 2 + 3 + \cdots + 100\) yig'indisini toping.
👁 Ko'rish
1
Bu AP: a₁=1, d=1, n=100, a₁₀₀=100
\(S_{100} = \frac{100(1 + 100)}{2}\)
2
Hisob
\(S_{100} = \frac{100 \cdot 101}{2} = 5050\)
Gauss formasi — mashhur misol!
JAVOB:\(S_{100} = 5050\)
💡 Tarix: 10 yoshli Carl Gauss bu formulani bir zumda topgan deyishadi!
