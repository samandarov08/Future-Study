Tenses

01 Present Simple
Hozirgi zamon ›

Present Simple — doimiy harakatlar, odatlar va umumiy haqiqatlar uchun ishlatiladi.

(+) Subject + V1 / V1+s (he/she/it)
(-) Subject + do/does + not + V1
(?) Do/Does + Subject + V1?
MisolTarjima
I study every day.Men har kuni o'qiyman.
She works in a hospital.U kasalxonada ishlaydi.
They don't eat meat.Ular go'sht yemaydi.
Does he speak English?U inglizcha gapiradi?
always, usually, often, sometimes, never, every day — bu so'zlar Present Simple bilan ko'p ishlatiladi.
02 Present Continuous
Hozir bo'layotgan ›

Present Continuous — hozir, shu payt ro'y berayotgan harakatlar uchun.

(+) Subject + am/is/are + V-ing
(-) Subject + am/is/are + not + V-ing
(?) Am/Is/Are + Subject + V-ing?
MisolTarjima
I am reading a book now.Men hozir kitob o'qiyapman.
She is not sleeping.U uxlamayapti.
Are they playing football?Ular futbol o'ynayaptimi?
We are learning English.Biz ingliz tilini o'rganmoqdamiz.
now, at the moment, currently, right now — bu so'zlar Present Continuous bilan ishlatiladi.
03 Past Simple
O'tgan zamon ›

Past Simple — o'tgan zamonda tugallangan harakatlar uchun.

(+) Subject + V2 (regular: V+ed)
(-) Subject + did not + V1
(?) Did + Subject + V1?
MisolTarjima
I watched a movie yesterday.Men kecha kino ko'rdim.
She didn't come to school.U maktabga kelmadi.
Did you call him?Siz unga qo'ng'iroq qildingizmi?
They went to London last year.Ular o'tgan yil Londonga bordi.
yesterday, last week/year/month, ago, in 2020 — bu so'zlar Past Simple bilan ishlatiladi.
04 Future Simple
Kelasi zamon ›

Future Simple — kelasi zamonda bo'ladigan harakatlar, va'dalar, bashoratlar uchun.

(+) Subject + will + V1
(-) Subject + will not (won't) + V1
(?) Will + Subject + V1?
MisolTarjima
I will call you tomorrow.Men sizga ertaga qo'ng'iroq qilaman.
She won't be late.U kech qolmaydi.
Will they come?Ular kelishadimi?
It will rain tonight.Bu kecha yomg'ir yog'adi.
tomorrow, next week/year, soon, in the future — bu so'zlar Future Simple bilan ishlatiladi.
05 Present Perfect
Tajriba / natija ›

Present Perfect — qachon bo'lgani noma'lum, lekin natijasi hozir sezilayotgan harakatlar uchun. Yoki hayotdagi tajribalar uchun.

(+) Subject + have/has + V3 (past participle)
(-) Subject + have/has + not + V3
(?) Have/Has + Subject + V3?
MisolTarjima
I have visited London.Men Londonga borgandim (hayotimda).
She has lost her keys.U kalitlarini yo'qotib qo'ydi (hozir yo'q).
Have you ever eaten sushi?Siz hech sushi yeganmisiz?
They have just arrived.Ular hozirgina keldi.
ever, never, already, yet, just, recently, since, for — Present Perfect bilan ishlatiladigan kalit so'zlar.

06 Zero Conditional
Umumiy haqiqat ›

Zero Conditional — har doim to'g'ri bo'lgan faktlar va qoidalar uchun.

If + Present Simple, Present Simple
MisolTarjima
If you heat water, it boils.Agar suvni isitsa, u qaynaydi.

07 First Conditional
Real imkoniyat ›

First Conditional — real va mumkin bo'lgan kelasi holatlar uchun.

If + Present Simple, will + V1
MisolTarjima
If I study hard, I will pass the exam.Agar ko'p o'qisam, imtihondan o'taman.
If it rains, we won't go out.Agar yomg'ir yog'sa, chiqmaymiz.
08 Second Conditional
Xayoliy holat ›

Second Conditional — hozirda bo'lmagan yoki bo'lishi qiyin xayoliy vaziyatlar uchun.

If + Past Simple, would + V1
MisolTarjima
If I had money, I would travel the world.Agar pulim bo'lsa, dunyoni kezardim.
If she were a doctor, she would help people.Agar u shifokor bo'lsa, odamlarga yordam berardi.
Second Conditional da "if I was" emas, to'g'ri shakl "if I were" dir.

Majhul nisbat

Passive Voice

09 Passive Voice — asoslar
Majhul nisbat ›

Passive Voice — harakat kimga yoki nimaga qaratilgan bo'lsa, u bosh mavzu bo'ladi. Kim qilgani muhim emas yoki noma'lum.

Subject + am/is/are/was/were + V3 (+ by + agent)

Active: Someone built this house in 1990.
Passive: This house was built in 1990.
ActivePassive
They make cars here.Cars are made here.
She wrote this book.This book was written by her.
They will finish the project.The project will be finished.
He has cleaned the room.The room has been cleaned.

Modal Verbs

10 Modal Verbs — can, must, should, may
Yordamchi fe'llar ›

Modal verbs — qobiliyat, majburiyat, ruxsat va ehtimolni ifodalaydi. Keyin har doim V1 keladi.

ModalMa'nosiMisol
canQila oladiI can swim. — Men suzishni bilaman.
can'tQila olmaydiShe can't drive. — U mashina haydashni bilmaydi.
mustShart / kerakYou must study. — Siz o'qishingiz shart.
shouldKerak (maslahat)You should sleep early. — Erta uxlashingiz kerak.
mayMumkin / ehtimolIt may rain. — Yomg'ir yog'ishi mumkin.
mightBalki (kamroq)He might come. — U balki kelsa kerak.
wouldXayoliy / xushmuomalaI would like tea. — Men choy ichasam edi.
Modal verblardan keyin HECH QACHON "to" yoki "s/es" qo'shilmaydi: ❌ He cans swim / He can to swim. ✅ He can swim.

Articles

Articles — a, an, the

10 Articles — a, an, the
Artikl ›

Articles — ingliz tilida ismlardan oldin qo'llaniladigan maxsus so'zlar. 3 ta article mavjud: a, an va the. Ba'zan article umuman ishlatilmaydi.

a + undosh tovush bilan boshlanadigan so'z  →  a book, a cat, a university
an + unli tovush bilan boshlanadigan so'z   →  an apple, an hour, an egg
the + ma'lum / aniq / yagona narsa         →  the sun, the best, the Nile
—  + umumiy tushuncha / ism / ko'plik     →  Life is short. Dogs are cute.
ArticleQachonMisol
aBirinchi marta tilga olinayotgan, noaniq (undosh tovush)I saw a dog. She is a teacher.
anBirinchi marta tilga olinayotgan, noaniq (unli tovush)An apple. An honest man. An hour.
theAvval tilga olingan, ma'lum, yagona narsalar, superlativeThe dog was big. The sun. The best.
—Umumiy tushuncha, ism, ko'plikI love music. Cats are friendly.

A vs AN — Tovushga qarab, harfga emas!
